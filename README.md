# The Great Indian Wedding - A Tri-App Love Journey
# The Great Indian Wedding — App Ecosystem  Three linked web apps that guide users through the complete Indian love journey, sharing one account and a **live theme engine** that transforms the look and feel of all three apps simultaneously when a milestone is reached.  **Rishta** (App 1) is a culturally intelligent matchmaker — users build profiles, get matched via a compatibility algorithm with horoscope integration, and get curated date spot suggestions. When both people rate their date highly, all three apps instantly transform their theme and App 2 unlocks.  **Shaadi Sajao** (App 2) is a full-service wedding planner powered by **Varmala** — your own wedding concierge brand that manages vendors, budgets, guest lists, rituals, and décor through a single dashboard. When the first vendor is booked, App 3 activates.  **Jannat Safar** (App 3) is the honeymoon planner — it quizzes the couple on their travel personality, suggests destinations, and helps book flights, hotels, and day-by-day city itineraries.  Running through all three is a shared **"Our Story"** scrapbook that auto-fills with every milestone — first match, first date, wedding day, honeymoon destination — creating a single digital love story from start to forever.
